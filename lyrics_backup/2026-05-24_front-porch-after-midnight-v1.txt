[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the whiskey warmth inside your stare
The midnight breezes pulling at your hair
You pour your velvet silence over me
And nothing else on earth is meant to be

[Verse 1]
The wooden boards are creaking soft and slow
As shadows dance across the porch below
You pull my denim jacket by the seam
And draw me deep into a waking dream

[Pre-Chorus]
I count the moments waiting on your breath
A slow-burn fire taking what is left
You trace the faded lines upon my hand
And make me feel like I am a brand new man

[Chorus]
Oh, wrap me in the midnight of your eyes
Beneath the heavy, star-drenched southern skies
I am falling through the quiet of your soul
You take the broken parts and make them whole

[Post-Chorus]
Just lay your head right here against my chest
Let all the wild and restless wanderings rest

[Verse 2]
The town is sleeping down the dusty road
We are wide awake and letting go the load
Your fingertips are humming on my skin
I want to lose myself and let you in

[Pre-Chorus 2]
I count the moments waiting on your breath
A slow-burn fire taking what is left
You lean a little closer in the dark
And light a heavy match against my heart

[Chorus]
Oh, wrap me in the midnight of your eyes
Beneath the heavy, star-drenched southern skies
I am falling through the quiet of your soul
You take the broken parts and make them whole

[Post-Chorus 2]
Just lay your head right here against my chest
Let all the wild and restless wanderings rest

[Bridge]
I feel the whiskey warmth inside your stare
Shadows fall and echo in the light
A perfect silence pulling at my soul
This sudden fire is our destiny
A heavy magic taking full control
Say my name softly... Saeed tonight...
[Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)
[Echo] (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I have been waiting for this exact moment my whole life)
[Spoken] (Do not say a word, just let the silence speak)

[Final Chorus]
Oh, wrap me in the midnight of your eyes
Beneath the heavy, star-drenched southern skies
I am falling through the quiet of your soul
You take the broken parts and make them whole
